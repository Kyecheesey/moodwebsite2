Fees & Rebates

Transparent, fair pricing

We believe financial barriers shouldn't stand between you and quality mental health support. Below you'll find our standard fees and all available rebate pathways.

Standard Session Fees

All sessions are 50 minutes in duration. Fees are consistent across both our Hope Island and Upper Coomera locations, and for Telehealth appointments.

Session Type Standard Fee Medicare Rebate* Out-of-Pocket Gap
Clinical Psychologist: Individual (50 min) $240 $137.05 ~$103
Registered Psychologist: Individual (50 min) $220 $96.65 ~$123
Provisional Psychologist: Individual (50 min) $160 Not available for Medicare rebate $160
Couples Counselling (50 min) $240 Individual rebate only† Varies
Group Programs (per session) From $60 Group rebate may apply Varies
Psychological Assessment (full) From $1,200 NDIS funded (if eligible) Varies

* Medicare rebates apply when you have a valid Mental Health Care Plan (MHCP) from your GP. You are entitled to up to 10 individual sessions per calendar year under Medicare. † Joint couples sessions are not Medicare rebatable; individual sessions within a couples therapy program may be.

Funding Pathways

We work with a wide range of funding streams so you can access the support you need with minimal financial stress.

Medicare / MHCP

See your GP for a Mental Health Care Plan referral. Up to 10 individual rebated sessions per calendar year. Rebate applied at time of payment via EFTPOS.

NDIS

We support self-managed and plan-managed NDIS participants. Psychological therapy and functional capacity assessments can be funded through your plan. Contact us to discuss your goals.

NDIS website →

Workcover QLD

We are registered to provide psychological services under Workcover Queensland for work-related injuries. A referral from your treating doctor is required.

WorkCover QLD →

QPS / DVA

We provide services to Queensland Police Service (QPS) personnel and Defence veterans through DVA. Please contact us to confirm current availability.

DVA Mental Health →

Private Health Insurance

Many private health extras policies include psychology rebates. Check with your insurer for your annual benefit. Note: Medicare and private health rebates cannot be claimed for the same session.

Private Health Info →

Compensation Claims

We accept clients funded through motor vehicle accident, public liability, and other compensation claims. A letter of approval from your insurer is required prior to your first appointment.

Cancellation Policy

We understand that life is unpredictable. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both clients and our clinical team.

We require a minimum of 48 hours notice to cancel or reschedule an appointment. Cancellations with less than 48 hours notice, or non-attendance without notice, will incur the full session fee. This fee is not covered by Medicare, private health insurance, or any third-party funder including NDIS, WorkCover, and QPS — it is the client's responsibility to pay.

If you are unwell or experiencing an emergency, please contact us as soon as possible and we will do our best to accommodate your circumstances.
