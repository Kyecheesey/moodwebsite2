How It Works

Getting started is simple

We guide you through every step — from your first inquiry to your first breakthrough.

1

Book Online or Call Us

Use our online booking portal or call 07 5573 2200. Our friendly team will match you with the right psychologist.

2

Your Initial Session

Your first session is a chance for your psychologist to understand your needs and collaboratively design your treatment plan.

3

Ongoing Support & Growth

Regular sessions help you build coping strategies, gain insight, and work toward lasting change at your own pace.

4

Medicare Rebates Available

With a GP referral and Mental Health Care Plan, you may access government rebates. We also support NDIS, Workcover, and QPS.
