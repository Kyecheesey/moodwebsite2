Join our team

We're a growing, values-driven psychology practice on the Gold Coast. If you're passionate about evidence-based care and a collaborative culture, we'd love to hear from you.

Why Mood & Mind Centre?

We are one of the Gold Coast's leading private psychology practices, with two busy clinic locations and a strong telehealth offering. Our practice is built on genuine connection: between colleagues and with every client we serve.

We invest in our team's growth, wellbeing, and professional development because we know that when our clinicians thrive, so do our clients.

🌱

Supported Growth

Regular clinical supervision, CPD funding, and access to peer consultation with an experienced multi-disciplinary team.

🤝

Collaborative Culture

A genuinely warm team environment: we share knowledge, support each other, and celebrate wins together.

⚖️

Work-Life Balance

Flexible scheduling, reduced caseloads for new clinicians, and a practice that takes team wellbeing seriously.

Modern, well-equipped clinics at Hope Island and Upper Coomera, with telehealth options for added flexibility.

Current Opportunities

We are currently accepting applications for the following roles. If you don't see your role listed, please submit an expression of interest below.

Registered Psychologist

Full-time or Part-time Hope Island & Upper Coomera Telehealth available

We are seeking experienced Registered Psychologists to join our growing team. You will maintain a diverse caseload across anxiety, depression, trauma, and more, supported by regular supervision and a warm collegial environment.

Provisional Psychologist

Full-time or Part-time Supervised placement Both locations

We offer a supportive placement environment for provisional psychologists completing their 4+2 or 5+1 program. You'll receive high-quality supervision from our experienced clinical team and gain exposure to a broad range of presentations.

Clinical Psychologist

Part-time Hope Island Senior role

We are looking for a Clinical Psychologist with expertise in trauma, complex presentations, or assessment to complement our existing team. Involvement in supervision and team development is encouraged.

Expression of Interest

Don't see a role that fits? We're always interested in hearing from talented psychologists and allied health professionals. Send us your CV and a brief cover letter telling us about yourself: we'll be in touch when a suitable opportunity arises.

How to Apply

To apply for any of the roles above, please email your CV and a cover letter to reception@moodandmindcentre.com with the role title in the subject line.

Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis. We will contact shortlisted candidates for an informal chat before progressing to a formal interview.

Questions about working with us?

We're happy to have an informal conversation: give us a call or send us an email.
